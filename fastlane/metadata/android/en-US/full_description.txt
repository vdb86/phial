Phial is a simple, beautiful screen torch inspired by the Phial of Galadriel from The Lord of the Rings — a light to you in dark places, when all other lights go out.

It turns your screen into a bright light source and works directly from the lock screen without unlocking your phone.

FEATURES

• Lock screen support — lights up without unlocking
• Brightness slider — set the exact level you need
• Fade in on open — smooth brightness transition
• Colour modes — White, Warm, Amber, Red, and a fully custom colour picker
• Camera flashlight — shake to switch to the LED torch, with brightness control on supported devices
• Volume button control — adjust brightness with the volume keys
• Auto-off timer — closes automatically after 1, 2, 5, or 10 minutes
• Disappears from recent apps when closed
• Dark mode for the settings screen

PRIVACY

Phial requests only the permissions it needs:
• Camera — to control the LED flashlight
• Wake lock — to keep the screen on
• Disable keyguard — to show over the lock screen

No ads. No tracking. No internet access. No data collection of any kind.
